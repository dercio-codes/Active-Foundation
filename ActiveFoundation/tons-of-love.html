Did You Know?
  • The term “food insecurity” means that a person doesn't know where or when they’ll find their next meal. In 2013, 49.1 million Americans lived in food insecure households, 33.3 million adults and 15.8 million children.

  • In 2013, 62% of food-insecure households participated in at least 1 out of the 3 major Federal food assistance programs.

  • More than 4 in 10 children live in a household struggling to meet basic expenses, and between 7 million and 11 million children live in households in which they are unable to eat enough because of the cost.


One of the focus areas of the Active Foundation is poverty relief. This is very close to our hearts because many people we work with are living below the bread line and many hard-working South Africans struggle to make an honest living. Thus we are constantly on the lookout for what we can do to better the lives of those less fortunate.

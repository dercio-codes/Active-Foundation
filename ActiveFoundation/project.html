Zero Hunger - Sow of Hope

During the nationwide lockdown, many South Africans lost their income or may not have received a full salary. For many families, this meant they had to endure many nights without food. As a result , we responded to the lockdown by handing out food parcels. It wasn't long before we realized that our best efforts can not keep up with the rising demand.

Tons of Love

One of the focus areas of the Active Foundation is poverty relief. This is very close to our hearts because many people we work with are living below the bread line and many hard-working South Africans struggle to make an honest living. Thus we are constantly on the lookout for what we can do to better the lives of those less fortunate.

Quality Education

The Leadership Academy aims to strengthen leadership structures. The curriculum is based on the Dream Team Series - which focuses on the development of a team as opposed to an individualistic mindset. Topics include good communication skills, casting vision and assessing progress. This is available to both student leadership bodies and teachers.
